Financial Formula

\[ NPV = \sum_{t=0}^{n} \frac{C_t}{(1+r)^t} \]

Where:

  • \(C_t\) = Cash flow at time \(t\)
  • \(r\) = Discount rate
  • \(n\) = Number of periods
